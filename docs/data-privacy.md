# Data Privacy

This portfolio is intentionally privacy-first.

The case studies are written for public review, so they avoid publishing private, confidential, or operationally sensitive information. Some cases may be based on real workflows, but they are adapted and sanitized before publication.

## What Is Not Published

This repository does not publish:

- real company data;
- customer or supplier data;
- employee information;
- real invoices or business documents;
- real amounts or commercial terms;
- ERP record identifiers;
- API keys, tokens, passwords, or credentials;
- internal logs, screenshots, databases, or spreadsheets;
- private routes, file paths, or operational artifacts.

## Demo Data

Demo files are synthetic. They are included only to illustrate the shape of a workflow, such as a parsed payload, a staging record, or an audit result.

Synthetic demo data should not be interpreted as production evidence, performance data, accuracy data, or business results.

## Publication Principle

The public version of each case study is designed to show professional reasoning while protecting private context. The focus is on the business problem, process design, control points, and lessons learned.
