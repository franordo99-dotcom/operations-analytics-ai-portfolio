# Operations Analytics and AI Portfolio

This portfolio presents selected, sanitized case studies at the intersection of operations, procurement, supply chain, ERP workflows, process improvement, data analysis, automation, and applied AI.

It is designed for recruiters, hiring managers, and teams looking for a hybrid profile: someone who understands operational problems, can structure messy workflows, and can use data, systems, and AI pragmatically without removing business control.

For a short profile, see [About](ABOUT.md). For the operating approach behind the cases, see [Methodology](docs/methodology.md).

## How To Read The Case Studies

Each case study is a public, sanitized version of a workflow or MVP. The emphasis is on:

- the business problem;
- the operating context;
- the process design;
- the control points;
- the tools and methods used;
- the qualitative impact;
- the recruiter signal behind the work.

Demo files included in this repository are synthetic and are used only to illustrate workflow structure. They do not represent real invoices, suppliers, customers, ERP records, amounts, credentials, or company data.

## Privacy And Anonymization

All public content is curated for privacy. This repository does not publish real company data, customer data, supplier data, invoices, credentials, API keys, internal documents, logs, databases, or private screenshots.

Some cases are based on real operational workflows, but they are adapted for safe publication. Any demo data is synthetic.

For details, see [Data Privacy](docs/data-privacy.md).
