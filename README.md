# Operations Analytics and AI Portfolio

This portfolio presents selected, sanitized case studies at the intersection of operations, procurement, supply chain, ERP workflows, process improvement, data analysis, automation, and applied AI.

It is designed for recruiters, hiring managers, and teams looking for a hybrid profile: someone who understands operational problems, can structure messy workflows, and can use data, systems, and automation pragmatically.

## Professional Focus

I am an Industrial Engineer with experience across procurement, operations, ERP workflows, cost control, process improvement, data analysis, and applied AI automation.

My work focuses on practical business problems such as:

- reducing repetitive administrative work;
- improving traceability in operational workflows;
- designing safer automation around ERP processes;
- structuring data before it reaches core systems;
- combining human review with automation and AI;
- turning operational friction into clearer processes.

## What This Portfolio Contains

This repository contains curated public case studies. Each case is written as a professional story: business problem, context, role, approach, solution design, controls, impact, and lessons learned.

The goal is not to publish raw internal work. The goal is to show how I think through operational problems and design practical solutions that can be reviewed safely.

## How To Read The Case Studies

Each case study should be read as a public, sanitized version of a workflow or MVP. The emphasis is on:

- the business problem;
- the operating context;
- the process design;
- the control points;
- the tools and methods used;
- the qualitative impact;
- the recruiter signal behind the work.

Demo files included in this repository are synthetic and are used only to illustrate the structure of a workflow. They do not represent real invoices, suppliers, customers, ERP records, amounts, credentials, or company data.

## Privacy And Anonymization

All public content is curated for privacy. This repository does not publish real company data, customer data, supplier data, invoices, credentials, API keys, internal documents, logs, databases, or private screenshots.

Some cases may be based on real operational workflows, but they are adapted for safe publication. Any demo data is synthetic.

For details, see [Data Privacy](docs/data-privacy.md).
